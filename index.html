The StaG Framework

What is StaG?

StaG is our proprietary, transformative, analysis-driven framework for large-cap investing — designed to deliver consistent outperformance with balanced risk. It is built on earnings-based analysis rather than price, enabling us to identify opportunities ahead of market consensus across four allocation pillars.
